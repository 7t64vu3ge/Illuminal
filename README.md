# Illuminal
psychological puzzle horror game.
This game is a part of submission for game jam Genesis 5, held by AIT Pune. 
Jam Site:- 

### Story: 
player gets illusions because of sound by listening to TV

goes into dual world where things are a bit off (works differently as compared to normal workd).

after solving all the puzzles the player wakes up from the dream. 

### Controls
move - WSAD
jump - space 
grab item - left mouse hold
resize - just grab item and move far from wall
interact - E
Sofa - F

### hints
Sit in sofa watch some TV, explore the home and other areas,
by looking at TV (keeping the crosshair at tv press E)
